About Me

I’ve been a tech junkie since my early days. I got my first PC at 10, started doing “tech stuff,” and never really stopped. From the very beginning of my corporate career, I’ve lived at the intersection of building solutions and supporting them — and building teams that do both, locally, regionally, and across time zones.

I’ve also had the privilege of working directly for some of the world’s largest technology companies. That experience allowed me to collaborate with and support some of the biggest organizations globally, getting close to different industries, business models, and cultures — and understanding how technology plays out in very different real-world contexts.

I’m currently an Executive Technology Strategist at Microsoft Argentina. I work with leaders at the country’s largest companies to help them use data, AI, cloud, and security not as buzzwords, but as real levers to drive measurable business impact.

Previously, I served as Director of Data & AI for Spanish South America and as CTO of Microsoft Argentina, always with the same mission: making sure technology serves strategy — not the other way around.

Before joining Microsoft, I was Global Leader of Multicloud Platforms at EY, building products and services across hybrid and cloud environments in the U.S., Europe, and Asia. I also led the firm’s Global Unix & Linux team.

Earlier in my career, I led technology teams at IBM during the early days of Global Delivery Services, primarily supporting U.S.-based clients. Always from Argentina to the world.

I hold a degree in Systems Analysis from ORT Argentina and completed executive management programs at Universidad Torcuato Di Tella (UTDT) and Universidad de San Andrés (UdeSA), where I also served as a thesis advisor for the Master’s in Digital Business program. I currently serve as board member, technical advisor, and fractional CTO for several startups.

I’m an AI advocate and a firm believer that this is a world-transforming force that will change things for the better. That’s why I’m a frequent speaker, deeply interested in how AI is reshaping the way we work, make decisions, and build the future. I regularly participate in events and media — both in Argentina and internationally — including Forbes, La Nación, Clarín, Ámbito Financiero, Apertura, Information Technology, IDEA Management, Nerdearla, Campus Party, and AIwknd.
